RENEWABLE ENERGY SOLUTIONS

Pakistan has a huge potential for generating renewable energy from sources such as solar, wind, water and biomass.Considering the growing demand of energy in the country, solar energy can prove to be an effective alternative.We are offering complete soloutions and services in the field of solar.

Residential:

Residential users have distinct patterns of using electricity.Whether you are living in upscale housing societies where there is a continuous supply of power from the grid or in an area which faces severe load shedding.For clients in areas facing severe load shedding, we not only help you significantly lower your electricity bills but also ensure that you get uninterrupted power supply 24 hours a day! Our solar technology will power your home using the sun during the day and will turn into a UPS after sunset.At the other end of the spectrum are people who have shifted to gen-sets to power the entire household. These consumers do not lead a restricted life-style but in fact have their entire household running on petrol/diesel gen-sets. Using a Solar Solution these consumers can Lead the same un-restricted lifestyle with Generating power using solar energy, which is cheaper and renewable No hassle of re-fueling the system as it is self-sustaining,Cleaner energy with No air or noise pollution.The system is good for over 25 years.

Industrial & Commercial:

Many large businesses have already started using solar energy to run their industries and others are willing to adopt the same after the reduction in capital bill.Power generation through Diesel causes operational expenditure.Cost of fuel and maintenance of these plants causes the cost of production to rise rendering a loss in competitive advantages.Experts suggest that per unit cost us Rs. 18 on oil and Rs. 6-8 on solar.One-time investment in solar will serve you for multiple years with minimal maintenance cost,zero cost of re-fueling and Green & clean energy with reduction in carbon footprint.Solar Systems can be designed to ensure a payback period of as early as 5-6 years.And after that, the next 19-20 years are free of cost for the client as we use solar panels with a lifespan of minimum twenty five years.We provide Off-Grid and Hybrid solutions to our clients in the commercial sector, depending upon the client requirements.These clients may fall in any of the following categories:-

Offices:

We offer products that will ensure uninterrupted power supply to critical appliances such as laptops, desktops, printers, lights, fans, etc. We can even power energy efficient Air Conditioners and other equipment if significant space is available.

Banks:

As with any customer satisfaction driven business, it is imperative for banks to offer service where backend issues are not visible to the client. In current environment most bank branches are operating on gensets. Furthermore ATMs have to be up and running 24 hours a day. The high opex cost of diesel may be justified during the day when customers traffic is high at the branches, but there is no reason to incur such costs when only ATMs have to be operational after business hours. Some banks have switched to UPS systems for continuous operation of ATMs. While this is more efficient than diesel gensets, banks are still paying a high per unit price to WAPDA than solar. For this reason we believe that solar is the best solution for critical operations of the banks and their ATMS.

Healthcare:

Perhaps no other sector requires uninterrupted power supply more than healthcare. Currently all major hospitals, labs and clinics have significant genset costs. While some have opted for UPS systems for critical system backups. We believe we can help with that. We can offer systems that will significantly lower operational expenditure and use solar technology with existing resources to offer Multisource Integrated Systems (MIS) which will ensure power at all hours during the day.

Educational Institutes:

Educational institutes have high lighting needs. This often translates into high operational expenditure. We can assist in significantly lowering these operational expenditures by combining a variety of efficient energy products such as LEDs with our solar technology.

Solar Street Lights:

Solar Street Lights are powered by photovoltic panels generally mounted on the light structure.The solar modules collects sunlight and converts sunlight into electricity, which charges the batteries.At night the light turns on automatically, using the electricity stored in the batteries. Solar street lights are designed to work throughout the night. Many can stay lit for more than one night if the sun is not available for a couple of days.

Agricultural:

Being an agricultural country, Pakistan has a large number of tube wells installed in rural areas which run either on electricity or diesel Generators. Both these power sources are inefficient and very costly. The recent power crisis and high cost of diesel fuel has led many of the farms to opt for solar tube wells.
