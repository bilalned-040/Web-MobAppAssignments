INTRODUCTION

HISTORY:

SIMTEK® was established in 1999 with an aim to manufacture equipment regarding the safety of electrical appliances such as voltage stabliziers, under and cover voltage safety devices and repair services. Soon after we started manufacturing of UPS/inverters. SIMTEK® gained a good reputation in the market due to its quality products. Recently enormous numbers of UPS/inverters are circulating in the market. In 2009 we stepped forward to the import of Online, Line Interactive, Offline UPS, AVR, Solar Modules, Solar equipment and Dry Batteries followed by the after sale services.

TODAY:

SIMTEK® is a registered firm having copyrights and enjoying good reputation in the market by providing power failure/emergency power solutions and power safety devices.

AIM:

We are aiming to move forward and step into green energy solution to provide pollution free environment to our next generation.

VISION:

Provide our customers sustainable and perfect solution of alternate source of electricity generation with cost effective prices and after sale support.

COMMITMENT:

  • Providing quality oriented solutions
  • Educating our clients about green energy solutions
  • Providing long life designs
